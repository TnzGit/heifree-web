Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Overview

HEIFree ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. All photo and video processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos, videos, or personal data to any server.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Photos and Videos: All scanning, conversion, and optimization happens locally on your device. Your media never leaves your phone.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Usage Analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Conversion History: Records of which photos/videos have been optimized, stored as JSON files in the App's sandbox.
  • Settings: Your preferences (quality, skip rules, language, appearance) stored in UserDefaults.
  • Quota Count: The number of free conversions used, stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • Format Cache: A cache of detected photo formats to speed up subsequent scans.

This data is not accessible to other apps and is deleted when you uninstall the App (except Keychain data, which persists per Apple's standard behavior).

In-App Purchases

The App uses RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

  • An anonymous user identifier (not linked to your Apple ID or personal identity)
  • Purchase transaction data (processed through Apple's App Store)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility purposes)

RevenueCat does not have access to your photos, videos, or any content on your device. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Photo Library Access

The App requires access to your Photo Library to scan and optimize your photos and videos. This access is used solely for the App's core functionality. We do not upload, share, or transmit any of your media.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@heifree.app
