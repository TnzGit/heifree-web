How It Works

Scan Your Library

HEIFree automatically scans your photo library and identifies JPEG/PNG photos and H.264 videos that can be optimized.

Preview & Compare

Use the side-by-side A-B comparison to see the difference before converting. Adjust quality settings to your preference.

Optimize

Convert in batch with real-time progress tracking. Pause, resume, or run in the background.

Review Results

See exactly how much space you saved. Every photo's before and after size is recorded.

Free Your Space

Delete originals when you're satisfied. They stay in Recently Deleted for 30 days — completely reversible.
