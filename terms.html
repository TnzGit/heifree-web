Description of Service

HEIFree converts JPEG/PNG photos to HEIF and H.264 videos to HEVC, saving storage space. All processing happens locally on your device.

Free and Pro Versions

  • Free: Up to 50 conversions at no cost.
  • Pro: One-time purchase — unlimited conversions forever. Not a subscription.

Conversion Quality

Conversions use industry-standard HEIF/HEVC encoding. While designed to preserve quality, minimal differences may occur. We recommend:

  • Using A-B comparison preview before batch conversion
  • Reviewing results before deleting originals
  • Keeping originals in Recently Deleted for 30 days as a safety net
