Description of Service

HEIFree is a photo and video optimization tool that converts JPEG/PNG images to HEIF format and H.264 videos to HEVC format, helping you save storage space on your device. All processing happens locally on your device.

Free and Pro Versions

  • Free Version: Allows up to 50 photo/video conversions at no cost.
  • Pro Version: A one-time in-app purchase that unlocks unlimited conversions. This is NOT a subscription — you pay once and keep Pro access forever.

Conversion Quality

The App converts photos and videos using industry-standard HEIF/HEVC encoding. While the conversion is designed to preserve visual quality, some minimal quality differences may occur. We recommend:

  • Using the A-B comparison preview before batch conversion
  • Reviewing results after conversion before deleting originals
  • Keeping originals in "Recently Deleted" for 30 days as a safety net

Deletion of Originals
